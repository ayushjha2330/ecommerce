Our Philosophy

Crafted By Hand.

Frequently Asked

As each bespoke piece is meticulously hand-crocheted by our artisans, custom crafting typically takes 2 to 4 weeks depending on the complexity of the design and our current commission volume. We will provide a precise timeline estimate upon accepting your custom commission.

Yes, STITCH offers complimentary standard shipping worldwide for all orders over $150. For orders under $150, standard shipping rates apply and are calculated at checkout. Express delivery options are also available.

To preserve the beauty and shape of your hand-crocheted modern heirloom, we recommend gentle dry cleaning. Alternatively, you can hand wash in cold water using a mild wool detergent, reshape while damp, and lay flat to dry in the shade. Do not wring or tumble dry.
